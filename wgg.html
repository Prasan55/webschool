Expanding Knowledge & Skills

Primary Education Program

Our Primary Education program for Grades 4-7 builds on the foundational skills developed in basic education while introducing more complex concepts across all subject areas. We focus on developing critical thinking, creativity, and independent learning skills.

Curriculum Overview

Core Subjects

  • Nepali Language & Literature
  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies

Additional Subjects

  • Health & Physical Education
  • Moral Education
  • Computer Studies
  • Arts & Crafts
  • Local Language/Culture

Program Features

  • Project-based learning activities
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Science experiments and demonstrations
  • Reading enhancement programs
  • Mathematics problem-solving competitions
  • Inter-class cultural programs
