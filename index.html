Welcome to Shree Salme Secondary School

Located in the beautiful hills of Nuwakot, Shree Salme Secondary School has been providing quality education to students for over 25 years. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow through a balanced approach to academics, character development, and extracurricular activities.

Our Commitment to Excellence

We maintain small class sizes (25:1 student-teacher ratio) and provide ongoing teacher training to ensure the highest quality education for your child. Our faculty participates in monthly professional development workshops and annual certification programs.
