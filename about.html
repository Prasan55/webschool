Our Story, Our Mission

About Our School

Founded in 2017, Shree Salme Secondary School has been a beacon of quality education in Nuwakot, Nepal. For over 8 years, we have been committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow through a balanced approach to academics, character development, and extracurricular activities.

Our Journey

2017 - Humble Beginnings

Established with just 3 classrooms and 5 teachers, serving primary level students in the Nuwakot community.

2018 - Expansion to Secondary Level

Upgraded to secondary school status, offering complete education from grade 1 to 10.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Implemented computer education program and smart classroom technology.

2025 - Present Day

Serving over 500 students with 25 qualified teachers and comprehensive facilities.

Our Educational Philosophy

At Shree Salme Secondary School, we believe in holistic education that develops the mind, body, and character of each student. Our approach combines:

Academic Excellence

Rigorous curriculum aligned with national standards, taught by qualified educators using modern pedagogy.

Character Building

Emphasis on moral values, discipline, and social responsibility through various programs.

Practical Skills

Development of life skills and vocational abilities to prepare students for real-world challenges.

Cultural Preservation

Celebration of Nepali heritage while embracing global perspectives.
