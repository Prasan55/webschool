Building Strong Foundations

Basic Education Program

Our Basic Education program for Grades 1-3 focuses on creating a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and social skills. We believe that these early years are crucial for developing a love of learning that will last a lifetime.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Nepali Language: Reading, writing, and comprehension skills
  • English Language: Basic vocabulary and conversation
  • Mathematics: Number concepts, basic operations, and problem-solving
  • Science: Introduction to the natural world
  • Social Studies: Our community and culture
  • Creative Arts: Drawing, painting, and music

Our Approach

We use a child-centered approach that emphasizes:

  • Interactive and play-based learning
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Safe and nurturing environment
  • Development of social and emotional skills
  • Regular progress assessments without pressure
