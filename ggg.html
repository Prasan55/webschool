Preparing for Higher Education and Beyond

Secondary Education Program

Our Secondary Education program prepares students for the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) and higher studies. We offer two streams in grades 11-12 to cater to different academic interests and career aspirations.

Grade 8-10 Curriculum

In grades 8-10, students follow a comprehensive curriculum that prepares them for the SEE:

  • Compulsory Nepali
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Health & Physical Education
  • Optional Mathematics/Computer Science

Grade 11-12 Streams

Science Stream

For students interested in engineering, medicine, and pure sciences:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology/Mathematics
  • English
  • Nepali

Management Stream

For students interested in business, finance, and social sciences:

  • Accountancy
  • Economics
  • Business Studies
  • English
  • Nepali
  • Mathematics/Marketing

Special Facilities for Secondary Students

Science Laboratory

Well-equipped lab for physics, chemistry, and biology experiments

Computer Lab

Modern computers with internet access for research and learning

Library

Extensive collection of reference books and periodicals

Career Counseling

Regular sessions to guide students in higher education choices
